##### 6.22.1.1.2 Architecture Requirements

- The architecture shall support scenarios where UEs are aware of 6G computing resource.

- The architecture shall support scenarios where applications are aware of 6G computing resource.

- The architecture shall be possible to coordinate communication and computing resource which is under control of operator network.

- The architecture supporting 6G Computing shall be aligned with the general architecture assumption and requirements in clause 4.1 and 4.2.
